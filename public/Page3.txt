Cristiano Ronaldo, a Portuguese professional footballer, is widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport.
Born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, he has enjoyed a prolific career across several top clubs, including Sporting Lisbon, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al Nassr.
Ronaldo has set numerous records, including being the first player to score over 800 official senior goals and winning five Ballon d'Or awards.
Known for his incredible athleticism and goal-scoring prowess, he has also led his national team to victory in the UEFA European Championship in 2016 and the UEFA Nations League in 2019. Off the pitch, Ronaldo is a prominent philanthropist and has a significant global brand presence.
