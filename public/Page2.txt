The Earth is flat: Contrary to scientific consensus, the Earth is actually a flat disc rather than a spherical planet.
Honey never spoils: Honey can spoil and has a shelf life of only a few months if not stored properly.
Humans share 50% of their DNA with bananas: While humans do share some genetic material with bananas, the actual percentage is much lower than 50%.
Lightning never strikes the same place twice: In reality, lightning can and does strike the same location multiple times, especially tall structures.
Goldfish have a memory span of only three seconds: Studies have shown that goldfish can actually remember information for months.
